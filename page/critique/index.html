My Response

Robots are designed for assisting human and creating the more efficient working and living environment. The concern of the potential threat from implementing robot to our daily life has created an unnecessary panic to our society. We must inspect this concern in two perspectives. Will Artificial Intelligence replace the human’s role in making decision? Will robot take away the job opportunity from us?

Artificial Intelligence still cannot replace human in role of making decisions due to the current technology limitation. This concern of human being replaced by robot is possible, but we should not focus on complaining or panicking. Instead, we should redefine our role in this society. If you take a look back to 100 years ago, automobile and various non-intelligent machines were taking over our jobs as well. The fact is that throughout the entire human history, advanced tools are always converting our living style and the structure of our society. Now, if you consider robot as another advanced tool which has no difference between the inventions of cars, you can understand why it is just a nature evolution to the society.

Our true question is to consider how we can accomplish something better with the assist from those robots. Just as the car replacing coachman, robot may replace some of the current job position. Yet, the invention of automobiles has benefited us way too much.

In these two articles, they both mentioned about the job that can now be done by robot either partially or completely. This information is not to tell people in those professional fields to against with it. Those whose job has been taken away from robot should allow themselves to redefine their abilities and develops new unique skills. In this process, our society will enter a new era where human has been doing the same since the ancient time. Now, it is time that all of us should prepare ourselves for the transition.
